Space sheet v1 - tech/revenue treemap

Tech Revenue Treemap

Preliminary Findings

  • Tags need to be heirarchical or layered i.e. a parent category to roll up respective tags is helpful for dissemination
  • Some tags just don't belong eg - proprietary technology
    • These should be moved into a separate labels column
  • Some tags are too granular to be useful
    • We should move these into a labels column
    • These are to be utilized on a UI for filtering purposes
    • Judge granularity by examining the number of the companies in the final list that fall in that intersection
    • Be cautious with the above process though - not all low # tags should be eliminated because they might indicate a sector that does not have a lot of competition yet, but soon could
  • Support a drill down view into the treemap
    • This will allow people who are interested in say: Human Launch to explore that area further

Revenue Treemap
Revenue Co-occurence

Findings

  • The Revenue Co-occurence chart shows that most revenue tags appear to occur on their own
    • This is great for segmentation
    • The few revenue tags that break this mold (perhaps meaningfully so) are
      • Other Satellite Hardware and Satellite Design & Manufacturing
      • Light Satellite Launch and Light Satellite Deployment
      • Remote Sensing and Earth Observation
  • Use the Revenue Treemap to find sector/revenue overlaps that you do/don't like
    • For eg - The tag for Satellite Hardware has 11 companies that fall under the sector Infrastructure and 27 that fall under the sector Satellite
    • Lots of revenue tags that relate to Satellite are spread out around different sectors
    • It's great that sector and subsector are placeholders for now - because this gives us a chance to finetune those buckets and find inconsistencies, if any
